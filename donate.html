The CRC accepts no corporate funding, no advertising revenue, and no grants with conditions attached. We are kept running entirely by people who believe consumers deserve an independent voice.

Why independence matters: An advocacy organization that accepts corporate money cannot credibly advocate against corporations. Every donation from an individual member is what keeps this Commission free to say what the evidence actually shows.

What Your Donation Funds

Website & Infrastructure

Domain registration, hosting, and the tools that keep the Commission's public presence running reliably.

Research & Articles

Time and resources needed to produce properly sourced, evidence-based articles and resolution documentation.

Consumer Advocacy Support

As the Commission grows, donations will fund grants and support to aligned consumer rights movements such as Right to Repair organizations.

Operations

The administrative work of running a member organization — verification, communications, and governance.

Every contribution helps.

There is no minimum donation. Even small recurring contributions from members make the Commission's independence sustainable long-term.
