Who We Are

About the Commission

An independent, member-funded body dedicated to evidence-based consumer advocacy on a global scale.

Mission

The Consumer Rights Commission exists to hold corporations accountable to the people they serve. We publish evidence-based resolutions, support consumer advocacy movements worldwide, and provide a platform for informed, coordinated action by consumers across borders.

How We Operate

The CRC is governed by an elected council that proposes resolutions and sets the agenda. Verified members vote on resolutions and campaigns. All positions taken by the Commission are grounded in documented evidence — we do not make claims we cannot substantiate.

Our funding comes entirely from member donations. We do not accept corporate funding, advertising revenue, or grants with conditions attached. This independence is foundational to our credibility.

Governance

Leadership consists of a small elected council operating on a collective model — no single individual speaks for the Commission unilaterally. Council members are elected by the membership on a rotating basis. Voting on resolutions and campaigns is open to all verified members.

Membership

Membership is open to any individual with a genuine interest in consumer rights. Applicants submit a brief statement of interest which is reviewed by Commission staff. There is no membership fee. Verified members gain access to the member community and the right to vote on resolutions.

  • Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis
  • Membership is free
  • Members must agree to the Commission's conduct guidelines
  • Membership may be revoked for bad-faith participation

Contact

For general inquiries, reach us at crcmail.official@gmail.com. For membership questions, see the Apply page.
